One of my favorite anime series is called One Piece. With a 26 year manga serialization and the anime airing for 23 years, spanning over 100 volumes and 1000 episodes, this pirate-themed epic fantasy series has become the most sold manga worldwide with over 500 million copies sold. Although the sheer length of the series hinders accessibility for new readers and watchers, it is definitely worthwhile as it has been able to captivate many people through its well structured story, characters, world building, themes, and emotional peaks. The episode 1000 opening is one of my favorites as it celebrates this milestone with a special version of the first opening theme.
